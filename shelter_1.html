About Tails of Love

Tails of Love is committed to creating a compassionate community where there are no more stray, abandoned, or neglected animals.

They believe destroying adoptable animals should not be a means of population control and is committed to help ameliorate pet overpopulation by promoting strict spay and neuter guidelines.

Tails of Love rehomes these animals into approved homes through an application and selection process and provides proper guidance on humane treatment and responsible pet care giving and ownership including their physical, emotional and behavioral health.
