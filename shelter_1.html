Tails of Love Animal Rescue, Inc. is a Staten Island, New York based charitable, 501 (c)(3) non-profit animal rescue group formed and committed to creating a compassionate community where there are no more stray, abandoned, or neglected animals and where destroying adoptable animals is no longer used as a means of population control. Tails of Love Animal Rescue, Inc. is also committed to serve the needs of the community by sheltering animals, strays and other animals; and to help ameliorate pet overpopulation by adhering to and promoting strict spay and neuter guidelines. Tails of Love Animal Rescue, Inc. rehomes these animals into approved homes through an application and selection process and provides proper guidance on humane treatment and responsible pet care giving and ownership including their physical, emotional and behavioral health.
