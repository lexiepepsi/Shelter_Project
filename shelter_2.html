About Mighty Mutts

Mighty Mutts is an all-volunteer, no-kill organization dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating the strays of New York City.

Mighty Mutts is driven to help find a home for the thousands of stray dogs and cats who are forced to live in New York City's streets and alleyways. They strive to provide a home for these struggling animals and get them much needed veterinary care. Additionally, Might Mutts wants to ensure strays are spayed or neutered.

Mighty Mutts hosts weekly adoption drives every Saturday in Union Square from 11:30am until 5:00pm!
