About Sugar Mutts

Sugar Mutts is founded upon the belief that all dogs are created equal.

The rescue is based in Brooklyn. It was created by Amy Marciano, an ardent Pit Bull/Staffordshire Terrier lover.

Sugar Mutt's dogs are rescued from high-kill shelters such as Animal Care and Control in NYC and various shelters in the Southern US. The shelter rescues all different breeds of all different ages and particularly focuses on dogs that are most often discriminated against.
